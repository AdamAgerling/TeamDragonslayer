Noone in the history of the world has ever become as famous for slaying a dragon as Sankt Göran. He encountered a pesky dragon in the year of 1489, but was unable to slay it before it fled. He had a real problem finding the dragon again. That is, until he hired Team DragonSlayer to swiftly put together a homepage with a description of the dragon.
